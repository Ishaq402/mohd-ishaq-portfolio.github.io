About

I'm a Data Engineer with 3+ years building and operating ETL/ELT pipelines for fintech and compliance-driven platforms — most recently at CreditNirvana, where I work on systems that support business-critical debt collection operations.

My focus is turning manual, error-prone data workflows into automated, cloud-native pipelines that run reliably with little supervision — whether that's parallelizing jobs across AWS EMR, wiring up real-time monitoring with SES alerts, or designing quality-control checks that catch bad data before it reaches a stakeholder.
